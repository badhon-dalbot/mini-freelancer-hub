How do I get started as a freelancer?

−

What kind of projects can I find?

+

You can find short-term tasks, long-term contracts, creative work, technical jobs, and many other freelance opportunities.

How do I ensure payment security?

+

Use milestone payments and platform protection so funds are released only when both sides are satisfied.
